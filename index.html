7. Why Misreading Is Old

Great powers treated Korea as an object

For over a century, external powers framed Korea as a prize. Many Western narratives still repeat that habit.

8. Strategic Geography

Pressure on Korea is structural, not temporary

Korea’s location has repeatedly made it a high-friction zone between larger states.

9. One Crucial Fact

South Korea did not sign the 1953 armistice

The war paused but never fully ended in legal-political terms. That still shapes risk and policy in Northeast Asia.

10. Misread #1

North Korea is not a simple collapse story

DPRK is brittle, coercive, and dangerous, but capable in selective state functions. Better diagnosis produces better policy judgment.

16. Expert certainty can fail. Stay humble.

17. Working Model

Read Korea through three lenses

Security: unresolved war and deterrence.
Industry: advanced capacity and supply chains.
Society: local ties and lived culture.

18. Closing

Korea is not distant. It is already part of Wisconsin.

Getting Korea right is less about memorizing facts and more about updating assumptions inherited from another era.
