Getting Korea Right

Neenah Club · March 16, 2026 Matty Wegehaupt

Most Americans know two things about Korea: MASH and missiles. That frame made sense in 1953. It is now dangerously incomplete.

1887: Great powers "fishing" for Korea
1904: Korea crushed between empires

For over a century, outside powers treated Korea as an object. The habit persists.
